Saints Run Club exists to help everyday runners go farther—together. We welcome all paces, all backgrounds, and all goals, from first 5K to marathon PRs. Our weekly routes are thoughtfully planned and captain-led, with warmups, clear start times, and a “no one left behind” approach—we regroup at lights and turns and look out for one another. We train smart, celebrate progress big and small, and give back through community events and volunteer days. Safety and respect guide everything we do: call-outs for hazards, space for pedestrians, and reflective gear when needed. If you’re seeking accountability, new friends, or simply a reason to lace up, join the Saints—come as you are, leave a little stronger.

Saints Run Club builds speed and stamina—together. Join us for structured speed work every Wednesday at the Astoria Park track and community long runs every Saturday. All levels are welcome, from run-walk to Boston-bound; we organize pace groups, share clear routes, and keep a strict “no one left behind” policy with regroup points along the way. Our captains prioritize safety (call-outs, lights, reflective gear) and good vibes (warmups, cooldowns, coffee). Come as you are—leave a little faster, a little farther, and a lot more connected.
